# Oui Market — Figma Design Prompt
# Complete UI/UX spec from splash to payment success
# Hand this to your Figma designer exactly as written

---

## APP NAME

**Oui Market**
Tagline: *"Campus life, sorted."*
Sub-tagline: *"Post tasks. Get paid. Build your rep."*

Design feel: Clean, modern, trustworthy. Feels like Cowrywise meets
Fiverr — not a crypto app, not a bank, not a student project.
Students should feel safe putting money in it.

---

## BRAND TOKENS

Typography:
  Display:  Plus Jakarta Sans — Bold, 700
  Heading:  Plus Jakarta Sans — SemiBold, 600
  Body:     Inter — Regular, 400
  Caption:  Inter — Medium, 500
  Mono:     JetBrains Mono — for account numbers and codes only

Colours:
  Primary:       #1A1A2E   (deep navy — authority, trust)
  Accent:        #6C63FF   (electric violet — action, energy)
  Accent light:  #EAE8FF   (violet tint for backgrounds)
  Success:       #12B76A   (green — money received, approved)
  Warning:       #F79009   (amber — pending, waiting)
  Danger:        #F04438   (red — dispute, error)
  Neutral 900:   #101828   (primary text)
  Neutral 700:   #344054   (secondary text)
  Neutral 500:   #667085   (caption text)
  Neutral 200:   #EAECF0   (borders, dividers)
  Neutral 50:    #F9FAFB   (page backgrounds)
  White:         #FFFFFF

Tier colours (OuiScore):
  STARTER:  #98A2B3   (slate)
  BRONZE:   #F79009   (amber)
  GOLD:     #6C63FF   (violet — matches accent)
  DIAMOND:  #0BA5EC   (sky blue)

Border radius:
  Small:    8px
  Medium:   12px
  Large:    16px
  XLarge:   24px
  Pill:     999px

Shadows:
  Card:  0 1px 3px rgba(16,24,40,0.10), 0 1px 2px rgba(16,24,40,0.06)
  Modal: 0 20px 60px rgba(16,24,40,0.18)

Spacing: 4px base grid — 4, 8, 12, 16, 20, 24, 32, 40, 48, 64
